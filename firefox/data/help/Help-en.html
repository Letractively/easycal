EasyCal - Help
How to view this help page again?

Click the EasyCal icon on the top-right corner of the browser to open the schedule list, click the question mark on the top-right corner of the pop-up interface to view this help page.

What can EasyCal do?

EasyCal is an extension to help you manage schedules everyday, it can:

  • Quickly create a schedule by selecting a few text;
  • Help you manage your schedules with simple steps;
  • Pop out a desktop notice at specific moment to remind you of the schedule.
How to quickly create a schedule by selecting a few text?

First, select some text including the time and content of a schedule. Then, right-click on the text, choose "Add Selection to EasyCal" in popup menu.

Check if the time is right, and click the checkmark to save the schedule. If the time has not been correctly recognized, you need to set it correct mannually.

How to manage schedules on the schedule list interface of EasyCal?

The user interface and functions of buttons are as below.

Click the triangle on the left side of schedule item to unfold the change schedule interface as below. After changing the settings of the schedule, click the triangle to fold it and save the new schedule.

Click the plus mark on left side of the banner "Create New Schedule" to unfold the create-schedule interface. After filling the form, click the plus mark to save the new schedule, if you don't want to create a new schedule, just click the cross mark on the right side to cancel it.
